How does it work?

This app is created to help practising conjugation of Japanese verbs and adjectives. To start, check the forms you want to practise. For example, if you want to practice the te-form of verbs, select 'Verbs' and 'Te-form' and leave all other checkmarks blank.

After you click the 'Start practice' button, you will see the questions. On top of the screen you will get the Japanese word with the English meaning. Below that is given what you should answer. Type your answer in the field marked '答え'. The app will accept answers given in hiragana only, or mixed kanji/hiragana/katakana. When using an western keyboard, what you type is automatically converted to hiragana.

After answering, the answer field will become green if you answered correctly or it will become red when the answer was not correct. The correct answer will be given below. If you press enter again, you can answer the next question. After the questions you will see a summary where you can see how well you did.
